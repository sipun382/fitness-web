Besan Chilla

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • 1 tbsp oil

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mix besan, onions, tomatoes, green chili, turmeric, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter.
  3. Heat a pan, grease with oil, and pour a ladle of batter.
  4. Cook on medium flame until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve hot with green chutney.
